About Funny Money

Funny Money is a fun and interactive mobile application designed to teach kids essential financial skills. Our goal is to make learning about money engaging and accessible for children of all ages.

Features

Interactive Lessons

Engaging lessons that teach financial concepts through games and activities.

Real-life Scenarios

Kids learn to apply financial knowledge in real-world situations.

Parental Controls

Parents can track progress and customize learning experiences for their children.
